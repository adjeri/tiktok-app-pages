Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • TikTok authentication tokens (stored securely on your device)
  • Video files you choose to upload
  • Captions and metadata you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf
  • Upload videos to your TikTok account as requested
  • Store your preferences locally

3. Data Storage

All authentication credentials are stored locally on your device. We do not store your TikTok credentials on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use TikTok's official Content Publishing API. Your use is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time by disconnecting your TikTok account or deleting the application.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact us.
