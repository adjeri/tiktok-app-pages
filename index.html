OpenClaw TikTok Uploader

An automated content publishing tool for TikTok creators

About

OpenClaw TikTok Uploader helps creators schedule and publish video content directly to their TikTok accounts through TikTok's official Content Publishing API.

Users connect their TikTok account via OAuth, upload videos with captions and hashtags, and publish directly to their TikTok profile — all from a simple interface.

How It Works

  1. Connect — Sign in with your TikTok account via secure OAuth 2.0
  2. Upload — Select a video file and add your caption, hashtags, and settings
  3. Publish — Post directly to your TikTok profile with one click
